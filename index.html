A Strong Argument

Hi!
I’m Chatchawan, a student at Thinkful and a maker at heart. I love to create new things from cast-off parts, giving them new life. I’m exploring my latest new frontier: JavaScript. And all that implies, of course, such as front end web development and the wider ocean of coding.
I had my start in GML, or Game Maker Language. Built for Game Maker, it’s a C# derived language that helped me ease into other languages. I started with drag-and-drop elements, then moved on to actual code once I found the limits. I have folder after folder of half-started GML projects, and someday I may revisit those fond memories.
Earlier than that, I had roots in other interesting programs, like Sketchup, Maya, Adobe Audition, Photoshop, and Bryce.
